How to Play

Welcome to Countdown! Here are the rules:

  • Click "Add Consonant" or "Add Vowel" to select letters. You can choose up to 9 letters.
  • Once 9 letters are chosen, a 30-second countdown will start.
  • During the countdown, form the longest word you can using the selected letters.
  • Type your word in the input field and click "Submit Word" or press Enter.
  • Each valid word will be scored based on its length and displayed in the word list.
  • When the timer ends, the game will display your final score and the words you submitted.
  • Enter your name and click "Save Score" to save your score to the leaderboard.
  • Click "Play Again" to start a new game.
